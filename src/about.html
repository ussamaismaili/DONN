How to Use

  1. Adding a Task: Enter your task in the "Add to your list..." input field and press the "Enter" button.
  2. Marking a Task as Completed: Click the checkbox next to the task to mark it as completed.
  3. Editing a Task: Click on the task description to edit it. Press "Enter" to save changes.
  4. Deleting a Task: Click the trash bin icon next to the task to delete it.
  5. Clearing Completed Tasks: Click the "Clear all completed" button to remove all completed tasks from the list.
